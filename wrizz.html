Build. Grow. Serve.

At Instrument, we envision a future where society and systems are just and inclusive, enabling all people to reach their full potential. In 2023, as part of our Build. Grow. Serve. program, we’re investing over $1 million of pro bono work to nonprofit organizations that support and empower Black and systemically excluded communities.








































Build.












Our Pro Bono Program

Last year we partnered with BlackSpace — a collective working to create a future where Black people, Black spaces, and Black culture thrive — to create an evolved brand, design system, and a reimagined digital experience that empowers them to grow their mission-driven organization into the future.








































Grow.












Our Donation Match Program

To support grassroots change in our communities, Instrument matches donations made by employees to non-profit organizations. Last year, we donated a total of over $180,000 to over 100+ organizations.








































Serve.












Our Volunteer Program

We give every employee 16 hours of paid time each year to volunteer with nonprofits of their choosing—collectively contributing over 5,000 hours to our community.
